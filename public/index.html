Who are we?


Here's who we are, and here is what we do.

The Newton Education Foundation is working to create a culture of support for education in Newton County, GA. Our mission is straight-forward: We're building a private fund for public education. But our vision is big: We want to create a better community. We believe that a great community values great education, and that great education inspires a great community.
